Consumer or producer?

Posted 11/2/2021

I think a lot about producing vs. consuming. I wonder which one I should and even want to be doing and why.

First, let me define these two terms the way I see it:

Producing

Producting is to make something. It can be ideas, food, code, drawings etc. Producing something is a creative process, one could consider all objects being produced as art.

Consuming

It is simply so easy to consume. I find it extremely attractive. After a hard day of work at the office (where I mostly produce I guess?), to fuse with the couch and consume some dumb shit tv. In rare moments I do find inspiriation to produce
